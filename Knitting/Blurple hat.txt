Cast on 144 stitches

For brim:
	*K1 P1*
Repeat for an inch or two

For body:
	Odd rows: *K1 P1*
	Even rows: K 
Repeat until the whole cylinder measures about 8"

	Odd rows: k[n], k2t
	Even rows: k
	Where [n] starts at 10 and decreaes at each pair of rows

	Odd rows: *k1 p1* repeat [n] times, k2t, mark the decrease
