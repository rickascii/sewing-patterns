Materials:
2 balls Be So Wild Yarn; super bulky weight yarn; 6o yds per 2.5oz
US 10 (6mm) knitting needles
Yarn needle


Instructions:
CO 3 sts
Row 1: kfb, k1, kfb. – 5 sts
Row 2 (and all even numbered rows): k1, purl across to last st:
k1.
Row 3: kfb, k3, kfb. — 7 sts
Row 5: kfb, k5, kfb. — 9 sts
Row 7 – 59: kfb, knit across to last st: kfb. — 63 sts
Row 60: BO 3 sts and fasten off. *Drop 2 sts and unravel back to beginning. BO 4 sts, making sure to allow enough yarn to travel the width of the 2 dropped sts, fasten off. Rep from * across.
